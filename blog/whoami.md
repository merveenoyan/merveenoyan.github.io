## tech work

I work in Hugging Face open-source team. I have developed features for, documented, yapped about a variety of libraries, primarily in vision & multimodality. Hugging Face is the type of place where what you _do_ matters. 

- One of the favorite parts of my job is getting access to shiny models before they're released, testing or training them makes me realize how far we progress in open-science every time. You can find some of them in my [Hugging Face Articles](https://huggingface.co/merve/activity/articles).
- Sometimes paradigm changes, I like to fill in and work on interesting problems. For instance early 2025 I integrated vision capabilities to smolagents and which unlocked a light open agentic web browser ([read here](https://huggingface.co/blog/smolagents-can-see)).
